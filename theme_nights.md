# Theme Nights

The following are ideas for theme nights the party could engage in. These are not set insto

### Masquerade Night:

All members wear decorative masks to hide their identities

### Queen's Night:

Women are in control, all men in attendance follow the Whore of the Night rule set and must provide for the needs of their magisty. *note women can choose to be whores on these nights too

### King's Night:

Men are in control, all women in attendance follow the Whore of the Night rule set and must provide for the needs of their magisty. *note men can choose to be whores on these nights too 

### Clockwork Orgy:

All members pair off in a large circle and have sex with their partner. After a set amount of time everyone switches with the person to their left

### Sex Bingo:

All members are assigned a number or a letter, like in bingo a combination of the two is randomly generated. those who have the corresponding number or letter will have sex.

### Sexy Talent Show

Groups of members take the stage to preform acts of wild sexual talent 

### Birthday Nights: 

Everyone present must service the birthday person in some way that night
